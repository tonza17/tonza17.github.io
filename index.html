About Us

We aim to develop a printed neural circuit board (PNCB) - technology that builds neural circuits with controlled structure. We will achieve this by:

  • Optimizing stem cell differentiation into different neuronal types
  • Positioning individual cells in specified locations
  • Making neurons to grow axons in a specified direction and synapse with specified neurons

Currently, drugs affecting neural functions are screened and/or tested using animal models, cell cultures or organoids. While animal models do not account for individual patients, cell cultures and organoids do not allow working with neural circuits with exact (precise) structure. Our technology will provide such solution.
